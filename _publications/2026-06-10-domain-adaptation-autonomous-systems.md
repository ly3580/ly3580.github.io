Domain adaptation is essential for autonomous systems operating under shifts in appearance, sensors, semantics, embodiment, and simulator-to-real dynamics. This survey introduces a bottleneck-knowledge taxonomy that connects distribution discrepancy, label shift, and data scarcity with statistical, representational, conceptual, and meta knowledge. It reviews applications across perception, prediction, decision-making, planning, control, and end-to-end autonomy, while highlighting safety-critical deployment, benchmarks, open-source resources, and future research directions.
